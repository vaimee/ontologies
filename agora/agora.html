This ontology designed for farmers to monitor and optimize irrigation procedures and, in the future, other activities related to precision agriculture.

This document is a work in progress

Introduction

"This ontology manages the relationship between weather observations, crop growth parameters, and soil horizons for precision irrigation."

InvalidSoilTexture

IRI: http://onto.vaimee.com/agora#InvalidSoilTexture

Auxiliary class used only as the head of a SWRL integrity-constraint rule: a SoilTexture individual is inferred to belong to this class when its clay, sand and silt percentages do not sum to 100. Since this class is disjoint with SoilTexture, any individual inferred to be both makes the ontology inconsistent under a rule-aware reasoner (e.g. Pellet).
